Easy Brazilian Brigadeiro Recipe

Want new cultural cooking experiences?

Try this super easy Brazilian Brigadeiro recipe!

Great for "sweet tooths" and beginners.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 can (7 oz) heavy cream
  • Sprinkles of your choice

See Ingredients

Mix

all the ingredients on a pan in medium heat

Stir

ingredients until the mixture thickens


Serve

Combine with sprinkles of your choice
